Armand Joseph Villanueva

President and Founder

Armand Villanueva is an inspiring leader, artist, and entrepreneur. He was one of the writers and executives of Bravo Filipino Magazine, which has highlighted Filipino excellence for over 14 years. He was the proponent and the man behind the success of the Bravo Filipino Awards, an event held last June 13, 2023 at the historical Manila Hotel to celebrate outstanding Filipinos who inspire through their contributions.


In 2023, garnering multiple awards in both National and International Level, he was also chosen by the Leadership in Education Academy and Development- Phils. (LEAD-Philippines) as one of the 100 Most Influential LEADers (Tourism Education). Furthermore, nominated by His Nobility, Royalty and Highness Prince of KUTAI MULAWARWAN KINGDOM in Indonesia, the prestigious MAHATMA GANDHI PEACE PRIZE was conferred to him in the same year by WAC (World Againts Corruption) People Council in India and the Philippines for his advocacy in Self-improvement and People Empowerment.


He also became the President of JAV Talent Unlimited Foundation, which supports young artistic talents. Currently, he is an active member of the Balanga City Lions Club International. Moreover, Armand is a founding member of TSKP (Tagapagtaguyod ng Sining at Kultura ng Pilipinas), an organization dedicated to promoting Philippine culture and the arts.

Notable Achievements:
  • Mister Elite Philippines International 2023-present
  • Man of Influence: Most Influential Young Leader of the Millennium & 2022 Best Dressed Male (5th Asia Pacific Luminare Awards)
  • Young Entrepreneur of the Year (2nd South East Asian International Achievement Awards)
  • People's Choice Award & Outstanding Entrepreneur of the Year (16th Gawad Filipino)
  • Gold Award Winner (Solo Voice Category, 1st Philippine International Arts Festival & Competition)
  • Mahatma Gandhi Peace Prize recipient
