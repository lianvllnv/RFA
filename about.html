Mission, Vision, Core Values

Mission

We aim to recognize and uplift the achievements of rising Filipinos who are making significant contributions in their fields, honoring their resilience, creativity, and innovation.

As a charitable institution, we are committed to supporting underrepresented sectors by providing opportunities and resources to help them inspire and drive positive change in their communities and beyond.

Vision

To be the flagship platform for celebrating and empowering the new generation of Filipino leaders - a new breed of public servants, emerging entrepreneurs, young talents, and those from marginalized sectors while fostering a culture of excellence, inclusivity, and innovation that drives lasting impact and inspires future generations.

These core values reflect the Rising Filipino Awards' dedication to uplifting individuals, promoting integrity, and fostering inclusivity while advocating for charitable efforts that serve underrepresented communities.

Organizational Profile

Rising Filipino Awards is a dynamic award-giving body and inspiring platform dedicated to recognizing and celebrating the outstanding achievements, contributions, and talents of Filipinos who are making a significant impact in various fields. Whether it be in the realms of arts and culture, science and technology, business and entrepreneurship, community service, or any other noteworthy endeavor, our organization aims to showcase the rising stars and emerging leaders who exemplify excellence and dedication.


Our founder, Mr. Armand Villanueva, started eyeing for outstanding Filipinos in 2023 as the proponent and one of the head organizers of the prestigious BRAVO FILIPINO AWARDS in celebration of the 13th Anniversary of BRAVO FILIPINO MAGAZINE. It was held last June 13, 2023 at the magnificent and historic Manila Hotel. NOTABLE AWARDEES ARE AS FOLLOWS:

PAO Chief Atty. Persida Acosta

Singer-Celebrity Jose Mari Chan

National Artist Fides Cuyugan

Businessman & Philanthropist Chavit Singson


This year, in partnership with Balanga City Lions Club and Gintong Palad at Balikatan Foundation, he wants to introduce the new bloods and give chance to the rising ones at NOVOTEL in Quezon City. Rising Filipino Awards will serve as a hub for sharing success stories, inspiring narratives, and motivational content that highlights the resilience, creativity, and innovation of Filipinos across the globe. Through engaging posts, interviews, and features, the Rising Filipino Awards strives to build a supportive community that uplifts and empowers individuals to pursue their dreams and aspirations.


Our organization fosters a sense of pride, unity, and encouragement, creating a space where Filipinos can come together to celebrate each other's achievements and inspire the next generation of leaders and trailblazers. Join the community and be part of the movement that recognizes and amplifies the incredible stories of the Rising Filipinos!
