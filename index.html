The Rising Filipino Awards Trophy

Symbolism of the Trophy

Philippine Eagle: The eagle, as the national bird of the Philippines, symbolizes strength, courage, and resilience. It reflects the determination of Filipinos to rise above challenges and soar to new heights of achievement and success.

Three Stars and the Sun: These elements, inspired by the Philippine flag, symbolize Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao, representing the unity and strength of Filipinos across the nation. The sun signifies freedom, hope, and progress, values deeply rooted in the spirit of the Filipino people.

Round Shape: The circular design of the trophy represents the cycle of life and the global presence of Filipinos. It symbolizes how Filipinos, no matter where they are in the world, are interconnected and continually striving for growth and excellence.

Gold Color: Gold stands for prosperity, excellence, and a commitment to unending growth and development. It honors the outstanding achievements and contributions of rising Filipino talents who continue to inspire progress.

Wooden Platform: The wooden base upon which the trophy stands represents the desire for stability and groundedness. It symbolizes the strong foundation that supports Filipinos in their pursuit of excellence, emphasizing the importance of balance and steady growth in their journeys.

The Rising Filipino Awards Trophy

Together, this trophy embodies the Rising Filipino Awards' core values of resilience, unity, excellence, continuous learning, and stability.
